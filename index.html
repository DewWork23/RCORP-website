Welcome to the Robeson County Opportunity Settlement Project

The Opportunity Settlement Project is a comprehensive initiative designed to improve the quality of life for Robeson County residents through targeted investments, community development, and enhanced services.

Our Mission

To transform Robeson County through strategic investments and community-driven initiatives that create opportunities for all residents.

Key Focus Areas

Education, Healthcare, Infrastructure, Economic Development, and Community Services.

Community Involvement

Creating meaningful change through active participation and collaboration with county residents.

RCORP Members

The Robeson County Opportunity Recovery Project (RCORP) is governed by a diverse board of community leaders committed to ensuring that settlement funds are used effectively to benefit all county residents.

Dr. Sarah Johnson

Chairperson

Robeson Community College

Dr. Johnson has over 20 years of experience in community development and education.

Board Meetings

RCORP board meetings are held on the first Tuesday of each month at 6:00 PM at the Robeson County Administration Building. All meetings are open to the public, and community members are encouraged to attend.
